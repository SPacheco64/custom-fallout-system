Blind Sight. The ant can perceive its surroundings without relying on sight out to 10 feet.

Hive Mind. The ant is aware of anything an allied ant is aware of (such as the location of an invisible creature), out to
a range of 300 feet. It has advantage on saving throws against the charmed and frightened conditions.

Powerful Build. The ant counts as one size larger when determining its carrying capacity and the weight it can push, 
drag, or lift.

Pack Tactics. The ant has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the ant's allies is within 5
feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.
